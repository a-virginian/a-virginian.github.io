RICE cake

jump to recipe
This wonderful recipe can feed a decent amount of people and is easy to share. I first learned about this recipe when I accidentally invented it while cooking rice in a rice cooker. Due to my capability to just stumble apon it, it is evident that it is easy to make. While I have not done it myself, I imagine it would be a great way to prank someone by adding frosting.
Prep Time:
1 minute		 Cook Time:
20 to 40 minutes		 Total Time:
21 to 41 minutes
Servings:
2
Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups white rice
  • 4 cups water
Instructions
Step 1 Put your rice in the rice cooker.
Step 2 Pour in your water.
Step 3 Turn on rice cooker and let cook until there is no water left.
Step 4 Place the inner cooking container upsidedown on a place.
Step 5 Lightly pat the outside of the conatiner until the rice cake comes out.
