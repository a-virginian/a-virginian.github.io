RICE cake

jump to recipes

This wonderful recipe can feed a person and is easy to share. I first learned about this recipe when I accidentally invented it while cooking rice in a rice cooker. Due to my capability to just stumble apon it, it is evident that it is easy to make. While I have not done it myself, I imagine it would be a great way to prank someone by adding frosting.

instruction

Ingredients
  • 1.5 cups white Rice
    • 4 cups water
Instructions
step1 Put your rice in the rice cooker.
step2 Pour in your water.
step3 Turn on rice cooker and let cook until there is no water left.
step4 Place the inner cooking container upsidedown on a place.
step 5 Lightly pat the outside of the conatiner untill the rice cake comes out.
