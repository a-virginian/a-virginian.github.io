Zwiebel mit Französische Zwiebelsuppe Basis

jump to recipe
This healthy recipe made with just two simple ingredients not only has a great taste but is also easy to make. You may be wondering what it is. Well, it's an onion with a little extra flavor. I first learned about this recipe when I invented it. I was curious if I could make a simple onion more tasty than it already was. So I went to my cabinets and took out french onion soup base. At that moment, a thought came to my mind: what if I combined two great flavors. an onion and french onion soup base, and that is exactly what I did.
Tips and Tricks
  • Check it every 30 seconds when microwaving to make sure you dont over cook it.
  • You could substitute the french onion soup base for any other soup base.

Prep Time:
3 minutes		 Cook Time:
5 to 8 minutes		 Total Time:
8 to 11 minutes
Servings:
1
Ingredients
  • 1 onion
  • A pinch of french onion soup base
Instructions
Step 1 Peel onion.
Step 2 Cut onion into any number of equal parts.
Step 3 Sprinkle on the soup base.
Step 4 Microwave until the onion is soft.
